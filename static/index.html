ClimFire is a real-time forest fire risk intelligence system for Victoria, Australia — one of the most fire-prone regions on Earth. Powered by a Machine Learning model trained on 10 years of climate data, ClimFire predicts daily fire risk levels, maps endangered wildlife habitats, and estimates the environmental impact of fire events before they happen.
